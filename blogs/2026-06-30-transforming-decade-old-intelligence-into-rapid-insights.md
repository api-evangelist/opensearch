Max Security partnered with BigData Boutique to build SCOUT AI, combining OpenSearch hybrid search with Anthropic Claude in a closed-loop RAG system that only draws on proprietary risk intelligence. The implementation cut analyst briefing time 79% (from 2 hours to 25 minutes), saved 7 hours weekly per analyst, and rated 42% of outputs highly relevant.
